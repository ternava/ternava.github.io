About

I am a Computer Scientist with experience in both academia and industry. Currently, I work as an Associate Professor at LTCI, Télécom Paris, Institut Polytechnique de Paris.

My PhD and postdoctoral research focused on advancing software engineering, with an emphasis on software variability, configurable systems, product lines, software reuse, software builds, and debloating. I also have eight years of industry experience developing and leading real-world software projects.

My current research interests include improving software quality by eliminating unnecessary variability in software systems, as well as addressing challenges in designing, implementing, and managing the variability of software systems.
